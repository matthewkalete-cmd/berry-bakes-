Our Golden Story

At Berry Bakes, we believe bread is a love language. Started in a small kitchen with a sourdough starter and a punnet of raspberries, we've grown into a sanctuary for carb-lovers. Every loaf is fermented for 24 hours to ensure that perfect "shatter" when you bite in.

From our signature berry-infused danishes to our crusty sourdough, we bake with heart, soul, and the finest local ingredients.
